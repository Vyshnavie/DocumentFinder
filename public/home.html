In this project, we aim to bridge the gap between the user and the internet search. We plan to build a smartphone application that can input in the form of text or an image capturing text in a book or a poster or a street sign. These images will be processed to extract the text. The input from the application will be fed to our text mining engine that performs text processing such as stemming and lemmatization, tagging and chunk extraction, phrase extraction and named entity recognition. Then the search engines can be mined to get varied search results and perform document similarity methods such as Jaccard distance to determine the closeness of the text. The results can be aggregated based on degrees of similarity and present a consolidated result to the user.
